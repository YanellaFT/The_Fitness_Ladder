The Fitness Ladder

Play your way up the board! Classic Snakes & Ladders game but with an exercise twist.

Number of Players

Instructions: (drag to move)
1. Roll the die
2. Add the die together and move your player the corresponding amount of steps
* If you land at the bottom on a ladder, move up!
*If you land at the top or a road, go down.
3. Pick up a matching colored card to the spot you landed on
4. Do the exercise on the card
5. Next turn
