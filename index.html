The Fitness Ladder

Ever feel unmotivated to work out? Well you're in luck!
Play this game to work out while having fun! Based on the classic Shoots and Ladders game but with a workout twist.
Play your way up the board!

No workout will ever be the same as cards take input from your roll and each roll is random.

Number of Players

Instructions: (drag to move)

1. Roll the die

2. Add the die together and move your player the corresponding amount of steps
* If you land at the bottom on a ladder, move up!
* If you land at the top or a road, go down.

3. Pick up a matching colored card to the spot you landed on

4. Do the exercise on the card
* You cannot move on until you finish the card!

5. Next turn
