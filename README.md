# The Fitness Ladder

Ever feel unmotivated to work out? Here is a solution: Play... theFitnessLadder!!!

- Choose how many players you want to play with (yes you can play with friends to make it even better!), 
- roll the die, 
- move your player (watch out for roads and ladders --> roads = down, ladders = up),
- pick up the card that matches the color you landed on, 
- calculate how many reps you need to do based on your roll (don't worry, instructions are on the card)
- do the excerse on the card, 
- next turn! 

You must pick up a card, do the excersise and close the card before you can roll the die again. 

Each game will be unique, as die rolls are random, and each card takes input from your roll.
